EXERCISE

Exercise is a physical activity that helps improve strength, endurance, flexibility, and overall health. It can include activities such as jogging, weightlifting, yoga, cycling, or even simple daily movements like walking and stretching. Regular exercise is important for maintaining a healthy body and mind, boosting energy levels, and reducing the risk of chronic illnesses.

With its mix of cardio workouts, strength training, and relaxation techniques, exercise is suitable for people of all ages and fitness levels. It not only supports physical fitness but also improves mood, reduces stress, and promotes better sleep, making it an essential part of a balanced lifestyle.
